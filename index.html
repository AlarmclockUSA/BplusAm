Brilliant Plus Terms of Service

1. Ambassador Program Overview

Welcome to the Brilliant Plus Ambassador Program. This agreement outlines your rights and responsibilities as a brand ambassador.

2. Eligibility Requirements

To participate in the Brilliant Plus Ambassador Program, you must:

  • Be at least 18 years of age
  • Have an active social media presence
  • Maintain professional conduct in all brand-related activities
  • Comply with all applicable laws and regulations

3. Ambassador Responsibilities

As a Brilliant Plus ambassador, you agree to:

  • Represent the brand professionally and ethically
  • Follow our brand guidelines and marketing policies
  • Maintain confidentiality of proprietary information
  • Regularly engage with the Brilliant Plus community
  • Create authentic and valuable content

4. Benefits

As an official ambassador, you'll receive:

  • Early access to new Brilliant Plus features and products
  • Exclusive ambassador-only content and resources
  • Special promotional opportunities
  • Access to ambassador community events
  • Performance-based incentives

5. Content Guidelines

When creating content as an ambassador, ensure that you:

  • Clearly disclose your ambassador relationship
  • Use approved brand messaging and assets
  • Avoid making false or misleading claims
  • Respect intellectual property rights

6. Term and Termination

This agreement is effective upon acceptance and continues until terminated. Either party may terminate the agreement with written notice. Brilliant Plus reserves the right to terminate the agreement immediately for violations of these terms.
